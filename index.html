About

I have studied my MSc at the IFUAP, which has ranked at 1st place in physics at national level. My pre-PhD physics three includes Dr. Emerson Sadurní, Dr. Marcos Moshinsky Borodiansky, Dr. Eugene Wigner and Dr. Michael Polanyi. I am a student of physics with a strong training in the theoretical description of quantum systems. I have experience in experimental quantum optics, numerical solutions of Schrödinger's equation, propagation of waves in crystals, quantum optimal control for superconducting qubits, spectral design of [geometrically-engineered (meta) materials] materials by geometrical deformations. I have performed internships in the Quantum Optics group, at UNAM, Institute of Physics, at BUAP, and Atominstitut, at TU Wien.
